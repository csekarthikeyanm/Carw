About Us

Carwash a friendly mobile service at your threshold started in the year 2017 with an inspiration to simplify and speedy the process of car cleaning with a strategic process executed by our experienced trained students keeping your car a dirt-free.
We function in a convenient way to ensures with fresh wash experience of your car with an impressive outlook at a competitive price.

Car Wash Services

Car Wash is a brand which is literally going to change the way people think about car cleaning. It is a unique mechanized car cleaning concept where cars are getting pampered by the latest equipments including high pressure cleaning machines, spray injection and extraction machines, high powered vacuum cleaners, steam cleaners and so on.

SILVER WASH

Complete vacuuming of cars incl. seats and boot + Washing and cleaning of foot mats + Body Shampooing and washing including door frames + Tyre arches cleaning + Underbody wash + Engine hot water wash and dressing + Side doors cleaning + Dash board polishing + Car perfume spray.

GOLD WASH

Complete vacuuming of cars incl. seats and boot + Washing and cleaning of foot mats + Body Shampooing and washing including door frames + Tyre arches cleaning + Underbody wash + Engine hot water wash and dressing + Side doors cleaning + Dash board polishing + Car perfume spray.

INTERIOR CLEANING

Complete dry cleaning of a interior including seats (carpet/leather), roof, mats and boot, Dressings to all vinyl, plastic, rubber giving your car spotless, shiny, glossy interior + Engine hot water wash and dressing + Side doors cleaning + Dash board polishing + Car perfume spray.

TEFLON COATING

Our PTFE based Polymer paint sealant creates slippery, slick barrier that protects the paint finishes, reduces fading & oxidation of paint. It repels dirt & grime, accelerates water run-off to reduce spotting, smoothness weathered surface and removes scratches giving depth to the paint.
